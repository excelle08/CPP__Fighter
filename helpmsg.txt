   -- WELCOME --
 1.Use direction keys to move your shuttle.
 2.Press SPACE key to launch a bomb.
 3.Avoid bombs from enemies.
 4.Press = to stop BGM and - to revive it.
 5.Press 0 to stop sound effect and 9 to 
   revive.
 6.You gain 20 points for every enemy hit.
 7.You spend 5 points for each bomb.
 8.If you missed a enemy you lose level*10
   points.
 9.If your points or life goes to zero 
   you lose.
 10.Shoot bonus to gain life points
 11.Press Q to quit.
